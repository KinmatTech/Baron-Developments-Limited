About
CORPORATE PROFILE
To be a key player in bridging the huge infrastructure gaps in the national economy by raising an army of skilled personnel
BARON PROPERTIES & DEVELOPMENT COMPANY LIMITED, “BARON” was incorporated on the 2nd day of November 1993 as an integrated Property/Development Services and Construction Company.
By a special resolution of the board on 20th Nov. 2007 the company name was changed to BARON DEVELOPMENTS LTD.
In its Twenty-Six (26) years of operation, BARON delivered quality services to private and corporate clients, institutional and statutory organizations.
BARON is able to achieve these by drawing on a rich human resource base of accomplished professionals in construction industry, adequate equipment profile and stable management.
BARON Services in it's years of operation had been focused on development consultancy and engineering / construction services offered to high net worth individuals. corporate bodies and ministries/departments/agencies of the federal government of Nigeria. Recently, the company re-strategized to include allied services targeted at widening it’s scope of operations across the infrastructure and industrial development landscape.
BARON Construction and Development Consultancy Services, has been successfully delivered to many grade ‘A’ Companies and organizations namely, NICON, Insurance Plc, Central bank of Nigeria (CBN), Peace Mass Transit Ltd, Federal Radio Corporation of Nigeria (F.R.C.N), MTN Communication Nigeria Ltd. Federal Ministry of Environment, Ecological Fund Office (The Presidency), PRIMERA Investments Ltd, Cross River basin Development Authority, Lower Benue River Basin Development Authority, Federal Mortgage bank of Nigeria, Federal College of Education Obudu among other blue companies and high net worth investors.
