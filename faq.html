Questions and Answers on BARON DEVELOPMENTS LIMITED
From market research, we at BDG Realty - a trading arm of BARON DEVELOPMENTS GROUP, and the developers of Rose Gardens, Enugu have noticed that there are a number of pertinent questions in the minds of potential beneficiaries of our Housing Estate. We hope the following information provided will go a long way in answering these questions. For any further enquiries, do not hesitate to contact us on any of the following numbers:
a. Why should I buy from Rose Gardens,?
• You may desire to own your own house and stop paying rent.

• You may desire to leave an inheritance for your children.

• You may desire to have other sources of income (rental income).

• You may desire to utilize the National Housing Fund (NHF) loan at 6% per annum.

• You may want to convert the rent you are paying for your dependants to mortgage.

• You may be desirous of living within a secure, structured environment that is well maintained.

• You may want to save yourself the stress of buying land and building in a remote area.

• And so many other reasons…
b. Who is developing Rose Gardens?
• BDG Realty , the company developing Rose Gardens, is made up of a group of vibrant, experienced, and forward thinking individuals who have a God given passion to deliver quality services in construction, Real Estate Development and related disciplines. This passion has led us delivering several turnkey projects in and around the country.
c. What does NBP in your name BDG Realty stand for?
• NBP stands for ‘NOT BY POWER’
d. Does BDG Realty have any track record especially in Real Estate developments?
• Yes. As part of BARON DEVELOPMENTS LIMITED, we have delivered on the following:

o Rose Gardens,, Premier Layout (By Presidential Road), Enugu, sold out as at October 2011. A total of 133 Housing units.


• The group has also executed several serviced layout designs and contracts for infrastructure provision.
e. What is the relationship between BDG Realty and Federal Mortgage Bank of Nigeria (FMBN)?
• Federal Mortgage Bank of Nigeria (FMBN) is providing BDG Realty with Estate Development Loans and will provide mortgage loans through a Primary Mortgage Institution (PMI) for the purchasers of housing units in the Estate.
f. Which type of house will be suitable for me?
• We recommend that which you can afford. That is why we have made four types of housing units available in various options.
g. Can someone tell me how I can afford to purchase any of the houses at Rose Gardens,.
• Through the use of the National Housing Fund (NHF). This allows you to obtain a mortgage loan at 6% per annum which can be used to purchase any of the housing units.
h. What is the National Housing Fund (NHF) all about?
• It is a Housing finance Scheme managed by the Federal Mortgage Bank of Nigeria on behalf of Government and the people of Nigeria to which you are a part of via your monthly contributions to the NHF scheme.

• These deductions on your salary qualify you to be able to access loans to buy properties at Rose Gardens. The loan is given to individuals at 6% per annum which is stable through the period of the loan. The loan period can be up to 30 years.
i. I heard that it is not possible to get these loans you are talking about.
• It is possible. Over 80% of the houses sold at Goshen, Jubilee & Bethel Estates were mortgage financed i.e through the NHF Scheme.
j. What if I don’t utilize my NHF loan?
• Other people will! They already are doing that in other parts of the country.
k. Will I not save some money if I go to buy a plot of land and build my house
• If you consider the following, Rose Gardens will be cheaper for you.

o The fact that you are only paying a percentage of the cost of the property

o Cost of Constructing access roads to your house.

o Cost of Laying pipes for water to your house

o Cost of drawing electric wires to your house

o Cost of buying land that is in dispute and settling various ‘parties’ to the land

o The estimated final Cost of building the housing unit, especially as you do not have all the money now

o Rising cost of building materials and inflationary trends in the country

o Cost of arranging for security for your house

o Cost of living with people who do not have the same way of thinking as you do

o Cost of people barging in on you whenever they feel like

o And importantly, cost of your time in doing all of the above!
l. Am I sure that this estate will be completed?
• God Almighty, Who ensured the completion of Goshen, Jubilee and Bethel Estates has done it again at Rose Gardens. Rose Gardens, was commissioned on the 11th of July 2014
m. But I do not like ‘owing’ anybody!
• If you are renting an apartment at the moment, please come to terms with the fact that you ‘owe’ your landlord/landlady next month’s rental.

• Instead of paying rent each year to your landlord/landlady, why not pay about the same amount of money to the mortgage bank knowing that it is your house that you are paying for.
Remember, your rent increases but the mortgage loan repayment amount remains the same all through the years.

• The monthly mortgage sums are quite easy to pay especially as it is calculated at 6% per annum, on reducing balance. This therefore allows you to still build your dream house while living in your own house
